02 // PHOTOGRAPHIC WORKS DISPLAY: GRID_VIEW

I. Axonometric Discipline (2015-2018)

Description:
Shot with an ultra-high-resolution digital back, focusing on fragments of construction sites. The images cancel all vanishing points, presenting an absolutely rational parallel structure.

Academic phrasing:
"By violently correcting perspective, Zhou Zhi strips the viewer of the privilege of being a 'subject', reducing the architectural landscape to a pure matrix of power."

NO. AXON-01 [Pre-arrangement]
Description: Materials and components are organised in advance as standardised units, spread through space in parallel logic.
NO. AXON-02 [Silent Standby]
Description: Containers, rebar, and modular components are absorbed into the same managerial plane.
NO. AXON-03 [Operational Layer]
Description: Machinery and temporary structures appear side by side without perspective, forming a pure distribution of function.

II. Deliverables: 0.00mm (2019-2021)

Description:
Photographs abandoned tie wires, dried concrete fragments, and piling marks left behind on sites. Each image is accompanied by a precise "Material Inspection Report".

Academic phrasing:
"A forensic collection of labour traces. Through the lens, Zhou Zhi records the last remnants of 'nameless labour' before the system swallows them."

III. Silent Sampling (2022)

[ UNFINISHED / POSTHUMOUS ]

Description:
His last unfinished body of work. Shot during his quarantine in Shanghai in 2022. The frames contain only sliced window shadows and extremely fine airborne dust.

Academic phrasing:
"When the external production line stalls, Zhou Zhi turns his 'measuring instrument' toward life itself. This series foreshadows the systemic collapse of his individual body."
